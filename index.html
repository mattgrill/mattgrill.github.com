Briefly About Me

My parent's worst mistake was getting me a computer. My life was over; they never saw me again. I always wondered how things worked. I started with an old lawn mower engine in my grandparent's garage and quickly moved onto the VCR and eventually my computer. Once you realize that everything just plugs into something else you realize you want to make it do something different, solve a problem, or fix a bug. My first attempt at programming was a two-player tank shooter, complete with bullet physics, written in Microsoft Visual Basic. This is how I got into programming and I’ve never looked back.
